Today's day-ahead price

The wholesale price for each hour, set by the merit-order auction. Dips toward or below zero happen when wind and solar flood the grid — the cheap hours a dynamic tariff exists to exploit.

What smart timing is worth

One household over one day, including an 11 kWh EV charge, under three scenarios. "Smart" means charging the EV in the cheapest hours instead of the evening peak.
